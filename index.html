Information of Tarannum

Tarannum is derived from the Arabic word tarannama, which means your tarannum meaning to kick or sing. Dr Ruuhi al-Balbaki, an Arabic language specialist, notes that the root word for the word tarannum is the rannama, your money, tarnim which means "intonation, modulation, recital, chanting, singing and humming" which includes the meaning of intonation, singing and reading well.

Tarannum is a science or method of voice that is diversified according to certain processes of Nada, Rentak and Irama.

The tone is a sound level or pressure. The beat is a movement that brings sound to the sound. Rhythm is the sound or the form of sound.

Tarannum is a term used by RasuluLlah SAW. His Word:

ما أذن الله لشىء ما أذن لنبى حسن الترنم بالقرأن
رواه البخارى-

Which means: "All that Allah has permitted for that is what is permitted to his prophet who always shapes the song (tarannum) of the Qur'an."

The Qur'an is the origin of Allah revealed to the Messenger of Allah through the angel Gabriel a.s. gradually and conveyed to us in a mutawatir way and reading it is a worship. the Qur'an has a unique art from the reading point that requires a person to master the tajwid, tarannum, qiraat sab'ie, and others in order to strengthen the mastery of the Tartil reading according to the form and verses of the verse to be easily understood, appreciated and practiced perfectly. giving birth to a generation of excellent Quran.

Claims of warnings in the Prophet saw:

حسنوا القرءان بأصواتكم فأن الصوت الحسن يزيد القرءان حسنا

Which means: "Practice the recitation of the Qur'an with your voices, surely the only good voice can add to the recitation of the Quran." (Hadith History of Abu Daud)

The best Quran recitation is a reading that combines the sound of the sound, the agony and the zenith that attains the true dignity of the truth (Nik Jaafar 1998). According to Nurliana Mohd Shariff (2003), the tarannum of Al-Quran is not just the same as other singing songs such as Lirik Penglipur, Syair, Qasidah, Nasyid and so on even the Qur'an can not be doubted as the singing songs. This is clearly explained by the Prophet in his saying:

إقرؤا القرأن بلحون العرب وأصواتها وإياكم ولحون اهل الفسق وأهل كتابين

Meaning: "Read the Quran with rhythm (song) and in arabic voice. Do not ever doubt the Qur'an in the style of the song of the wicked and the scribes (Christians and Jews). "(Hadith narrated by Imam Thabrani and Baihaqi).
