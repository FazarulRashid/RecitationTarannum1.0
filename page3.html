Type of Tarannum

TARANNUM BAYYATI

The word bayyati is from Arabic which means a home.

Features of tarannum bayatti:

  • Soft and firm
  • Harmony
  • Suit with medium range

The role of Tarannum Al-Bayyati

  • Strengthen the presentation
  • Suitable for opening and closing tarannum
  • Measurements to tarannum afterwards
  • Can create a feeling of admiration of the Quranic miracle

TARANNUM SOBA

It means miss and popularized by the Egyptian qari.

Features of Tarannum Soba

  • Light movement permeates the soul
  • Suit with medium range
  • Suit with the verse pleading, seducing, moaning and so forth.

The role of Tarannum Soba

  • Enjoy the show
  • Bring awareness
  • Can create a feeling of admiration of the Quranic miracle
